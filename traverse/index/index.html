traverse遍历

Aug 7, 2019 | 前端面试 | Hits

本篇文章主要讲解了遍历：深度优先遍历、广度优先遍历。

遍历

深度优先遍历

深度优先遍历结果如下

0: div.parent
1: div.child-1
2: div.child-1-1
3: div.child-1-1-1
4: div.child-1-2
5: div.child-1-2-1
6: div.child-1-3
7: div.child-2
8: div.child-2-1
9: div.child-2-2

广度优先遍历

广度优先遍历结果如下

0: div.parent
1: div.child-2
2: div.child-1
3: div.child-2-2
4: div.child-2-1
5: div.child-1-3
6: div.child-1-2
7: div.child-1-1
8: div.child-1-2-1
9: div.child-1-1-1
